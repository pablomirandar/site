About

I am Pablo Miranda Rozas, Associate Professor at the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science, Universidad de Santiago de Chile. I work on problems in mathematical physics and spectral theory, with emphasis on magnetic Schrödinger operators, resonances in Quantum Systems, and discrete operators.

Research lines

  • • Magnetic Schrödinger operators: Distribution of eigenvalues, and spectral shift function asymptotitics.
  • • Resonances in deformed quantum waveguides.
  • • Spectral properties of discrete operators (Schrödinger; Dirac/Hodge-Dirac).
